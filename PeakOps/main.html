PeakOps

Seamless Auditing & IT Solutions

Business Overview

Mission: Provide seamless auditing and IT solutions to optimize operations, reduce costs, and ensure compliance for restaurants, retail stores, and SMEs.

Service Offerings by Vertical

A. Restaurants

  • Inventory Management: Track perishables, expiry dates, and stock levels.
  • Purchase & Supplier Audits: Verify invoices and pricing accuracy.
  • Recipe & Production Analysis: Monitor portion control and ingredient costs.
  • Waste Tracking: Identify waste sources and recommend reductions.
  • POS Integration: Audit sales data, menu pricing, and transaction accuracy.
  • Reports: Profit/loss, inventory turnover, waste analytics.

B. Retail

  • Inventory Audits: Stock reconciliation, shrinkage analysis.
  • Purchase Order Audits: Supplier compliance and pricing checks.
  • Waste Management: Track unsold/damaged goods.
  • POS Support: Sales data validation and fraud detection.
  • Reports: Sales trends, inventory efficiency.

C. IT Services (For Both Sectors)

  • Remote Support: 24/7 troubleshooting for POS, software, and networks.
  • On-Site Assistance: Hardware repairs, server setup, and cabling.
  • Maintenance: Regular hardware/software updates, server health checks.
  • Data Security: Automated backups, disaster recovery, and compliance (e.g., GDPR).
  • Custom Solutions: POS customization, cloud migration, and API integrations.

Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

  • All-in-One Solution: Combines operational auditing with IT support, reducing the need for multiple vendors.
  • Industry-Specific Expertise: Tailored tools for restaurants (e.g., recipe costing) and retail (e.g., shrinkage analysis).
  • Proactive IT Maintenance: Prevent downtime with predictive server/software monitoring.
